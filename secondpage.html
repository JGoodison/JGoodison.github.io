The Effects of...

Social Media

Negative Effects

  • Social Media can be addicting.
  • Social Media can affect your mental health. Comparison is a confidence killer and very easy to do on a platform where everyone constantly shows the best version of themselves.
  • Social Media (in addtion to other technologies) can strip you of your privacy.
  • Social Media can disrupt your sleep patterns.

Positive Effects

  • Social Media allows you to connect with people all around the world no matter the distance.
  • Social Media gives you a platform for activism allowing you to bring awareness to a topic you're passionate about.
  • Social Media is a home for sharing personal creativity.
  • Social Media can be educational.
  • Social Media can bring you joy and laughter.
